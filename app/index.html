HUSTI Manual
❓ Why & What is HUSTI? HUSTI is a private, serverless savings manager built to make saving habitual. No cloud servers are connected, keeping your financial logs 100% private.
💾 How to Back Up? Since HUSTI saves data locally, clearing browser cache deletes it. To protect your data, go to Settings -> Data Vault and click "Export Backup". Save this file. If you switch devices, click "Import Backup" to restore.
🖼️ How to Change Background? Click the HUSTI logo (top-left) in the navigation bar to open the wallpaper menu. Wallpapers are shown only in the Calendar view to keep Analytics and Settings views clean.
